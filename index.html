Our Story

Founded in 2024, Save2Serve emerged from a simple observation: while millions of people face food insecurity, tons of perfectly good food are wasted daily. Our team of passionate technologists, community advocates, and sustainability experts came together to bridge this gap through innovative solutions that benefit everyone involved.
